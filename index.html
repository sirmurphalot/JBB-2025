✨ Special Keynote Announcement ✨

A Joint Presentation by Alexander Craig Murph & John Michael Hailey

Alexander Craig Murph

John Michael Hailey

Topic: “November 22nd: Reflections on a Date That Shaped a Nation”

Join us for a powerful and historically resonant keynote examining the modern relevance and national significance of November 22nd. Our speakers will explore the pivotal events that have defined this date, offering a thoughtful overview of its lasting impact on history, culture, and collective memory.

This session promises to be both reflective and forward-looking — connecting past lessons to today’s challenges and insights.
