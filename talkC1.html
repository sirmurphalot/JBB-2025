"And Yet"

Presenter: William Macleod Adams, writer, director

Abstract: A 20 minute loosely scripted/ directed --lots of room for improvisation- play performance. The cast (chosen volunteers) will get there scripts at the beginning of the 20 min and have 5 minutes to review before performing. The play will be an exploration of a day in the life of a youth therapeutic group home- leaning deep into the comedy, love and grief of such a place. There will be minimal set design/props but there will be some.

Conference Room 72, 10:00pm
