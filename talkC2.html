How I Plan to Eat a Turkey that has Received a Thanksgiving Day Presidential Pardon.

Presenter: AC Murph is one of the birthday boys.

Abstract: There are very few stranger traditions in the United States than the yearly presidential pardon of a Thanksgiving-day turkey. Even fewer people look at that tradition and ask "why the literaly heck are we pardoning those turkeys."
What if I told you that this was intentional.
What if there were a reason all those turkeys were pardoned and no one every questioned it.
In this talk, I will discuss the history of the thanksgiving-day presidential turkey pardon, unveil a long history of state secrets concerning this pardon, and explain the why and how I plan to one day soon consume one of these turkeys.
